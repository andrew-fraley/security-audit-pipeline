**Step 4: Final Delivery**

**Purpose**

Deliver the fully reviewed and finalized assessment findings report to the client.

**Deliverable**
- Security assessmnent report
- Risk summary
- High-level recommendations

Note: The Final Report contains the findings report, risk summary, and recommendations typically in one concise document. Additionally, this is not a full risk management project, this is just a security audit/assessement, it identifies gaps and risks in the client's security posture, it aims to make high-level recommendations. What it does not do is provide a full contol implementation plan or policy development, this is handled in other projects such as the IRS Written Informaiton Securtiy Plan development.
